About Me

Hi! I’m Johanna Nguyen, a sophomore at Brown University pursuing a B.S. in Applied Mathematics–Computer Science. At the root of everything I do is a love for logic: it’s what drives my interests in machine learning, embedded systems, and computational statistics.

Outside the classroom, I’m the Driver Controls Lead for Brown Formula Racing, the Events Coordinator (and founding board member, soon-to-be Co-President) of Women Build @ Brown, and a Monitor-in-Training at the Brown Design Workshop. These roles reflect the values that matter most to me: collaborative system integration (a.k.a. good teamwork), community-based activism, and sharing knowledge.

Professionally, I’ve researched sustainable aviation infrastructure at Pratt & Whitney, collected data on police transparency with Sivil, and will return to Pratt & Whitney this summer (2025) as an Engine Systems Software Intern in Controls & Diagnostic Systems. I'm driven to use technology for good — working at the intersection of activism, transportation, and innovation.
